§1 Abstract

I am Rajesh Mojumder, a Faculty Member in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Canadian University of Bangladesh. I teach Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures and Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, and Structured Programming. My teaching approach combines conceptual clarity with practical implementation, and I work with both on-campus and online students to strengthen their problem-solving and programming foundations.

I am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University with a CGPA of 4.00 out of 4.00, following the completion of my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from the same institution. My research interests include machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing, with a focus on applying these methods to healthcare, retail, and language-based systems. My work has been published in MDPI journals and presented at international conferences, including ITISE (Spain) and ICCCNT.

Prior to academia, I worked as a QA Test Engineer, where I developed a structured and detail-oriented approach to software quality assurance, testing, and system validation. This experience continues to inform my academic and research work, particularly in building reliable and reproducible machine learning systems. I am committed to advancing computer science through teaching, research, and the development of practical, impact-driven solutions.

01

Student Exam Performance Prediction

Predicts a student's academic performance from demographic and educational factors, so institutions can flag at-risk students early and step in with support before the final exam rather than after.

Objective

Build a predictive model that estimates exam scores from historical educational data (gender, parental education, lunch type, test preparation, reading/writing/maths scores).

Workflow

Data cleaning & one-hot encoding → feature scaling → train/test split → model comparison (Linear Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, XGBoost) → evaluation on MAE, MSE, R² → deployment as a web app for live predictions.

02

Chicken Disease Classification

Identifies poultry diseases directly from chicken images using deep learning and transfer learning, giving farmers a way to catch disease before it spreads across a farm.

Objective

Assist poultry farmers with early, image-based disease detection using a labelled dataset of healthy and infected chickens.

Workflow

Load pretrained VGG16 → freeze convolution layers → add custom dense + softmax layers → augment & preprocess images → train, validate, predict → evaluate on accuracy, precision, recall, F1, confusion matrix.

04

Federated Learning for Retail

Lets multiple retail branches collaboratively train one machine learning model without ever pooling customer data in a central location, solving the privacy problem that traditional centralised ML runs into.

Workflow

Local store trains a local model → sends model weights (not data) to a federated server → server aggregates weights → updates the global model → returns it to each branch.

Why it matters

Privacy preservation, GDPR-aligned data handling, reduced data transfer, applicable across banking, healthcare, retail, and smart devices.
